# 🥏 Bingo Bango Bongo – Disc Golf Edition

A mobile-friendly scoring app for the Bingo Bango Bongo disc golf game.

## How to Deploy (Free, ~10 minutes)

### Step 1 – Create a GitHub account
Go to https://github.com and sign up for a free account if you don't have one.

### Step 2 – Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** button in the top right → **New repository**
2. Name it `bingo-bango-bongo`
3. Leave it **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 – Upload the files
1. On your new repo page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag the entire contents of this folder into the upload box
   - Make sure to include the `src/` folder and all files
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 – Deploy on Netlify
1. Go to https://netlify.com and sign up for a free account
2. Click **Add new site** → **Import an existing project**
3. Choose **GitHub** and authorize Netlify
4. Select your `bingo-bango-bongo` repository
5. Netlify will auto-detect the settings. Just click **Deploy site**
6. Wait ~1 minute — your site will be live!

### Step 5 – Share with friends
- Netlify gives you a URL like `https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app`
- You can customize it to something like `https://bingobangobongo.netlify.app` in site settings
- Share the link with your group!

### Step 6 – Add to phone home screen
**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" — it now looks like an app!

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the link in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
